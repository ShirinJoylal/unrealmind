QUANTUM theory

Explore the fascinating world of quantum mechanics, where particles exist in multiple states and the universe is governed by probabilities rather than certainties. Quantum mechanics is the study of the smallest fundamental particles and their interactions, revealing a reality that is both strange and beautiful. From the behavior of electrons to the entanglement of particles across vast distances, quantum mechanics challenges our understanding of reality and opens up new possibilities for technology and science. Even the electronic devices we use every day rely on principles of quantum mechanics to function.

Quantum theories are well known, accepted and proven experimentally, and they define all about our universe and how it works at the smallest scales.
