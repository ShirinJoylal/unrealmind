RELATIVITY

Special relativity describes how space and time are interwoven into a single continuum known as spacetime. And it shows how measurement of them are relative to the observers motion. General relativity is the geometric theory of gravitation, which describes how matter and energy in the universe influence the curvature of spacetime,how earth and other celestial bodies move in response to this curvature, and how the solar system and all mass in the universe interact with each other through this curvature. and it is well known and accepted and solves many unexplained questions. Einstien defines speed of light in his special theory of relativity (1905) as a universal constant, which is the maximum speed at which all energy, matter, and information in the universe can travel. This means that nothing can travel faster than the speed of light in a vacuum, which is approximately 299,792,458 meters per second (or about 186,282 miles per second).
