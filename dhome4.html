Theory Of Everything (TOE)

However the relativity theory and quantum theory are well accepted they won’t interconnect each other, they interconnect but not completely, so we are trying to connect them with a new theory called Theory of Everything (TOE).

Relativity describes the universe as a fabric of space-time, while quantum theory explains it through particles and waves. Scientists are working hard to unify these into one framework, which could help explain black holes, the Big Bang, and much more.

Quantum theory states that our universe is made up of particles and waves, whereas relativity states that our universe is made up of fabric of space and time. A unifying theory would solve two major problems: the big bang singularity (where the universe started from a singularity) and the black hole singularity (infinite density, zero volume).

Scientists continue their search for this ultimate framework that could elegantly describe everything in the cosmos — from galaxies and black holes to atoms and quarks.
